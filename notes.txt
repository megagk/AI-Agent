================= Background =================
What is an AI Agent?
A system that can perceive its environment, make decisions and act autonomously to achieve a specific goals.
It OBSERVES, THINGS and ACTS.

Example:
Siri
Perceives: Listens to your voice command e.g., "what's the weather today?"
Thinks: Converts speech to text and interprets intent using NLP, then searches for the answer
Acts: Responds with a spoken answer or performs a task. e.g., "The weather is 20 degrees today in London"
